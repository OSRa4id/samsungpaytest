3 Get Payment Credentials — GET /ops/v1/transactions/paymentCredentials/{ref_id}

After Samsung redirects to your Callback URL with ?ref_id=..., your server calls this API to retrieve the encrypted credential. Paste the ref_id and the credential response here.

4 Notify Payment Result — POST /ops/v1/notifications
